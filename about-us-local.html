Leadership

YD

Yinka Daramola

Chief Executive Officer

Yinka is a seasoned professional based in San Diego, California, with extensive expertise in software engineering, business process optimization, and intelligent automation. Holding a Master's degree from National University, Yinka has built a career at the intersection of technology and operational efficiency, particularly in healthcare settings.

His technical capabilities shine through roles as an expert RPA (Robotic Process Automation) developer, Business Analyst, and Technical Solution Architect. Proficient in tools like UiPath, Blue Prism, and Power Automate, Yinka excels at identifying repetitive patterns in complex workflows and deploying resilient automation solutions that deliver results in record time.

His skills encompass full-cycle project delivery, from analysis and design to implementation and optimization, ensuring shortened timelines, reduced costs, and maintained quality.

Healthcare Innovation

Yinka's healthcare experience is deeply rooted in primary care operations, where he spent 8+ years managing administrative burdens. By implementing RPA, he transformed a process that once took a week to prepare 300 invoices into a mere 5-hour task, freeing up to 70% more time for higher-value clinical activities.

This hands-on innovation is reflected in his thought leadership, including articles on RPA in healthcare, digital transformation for clinics, and key performance indicators (KPIs) in primary care. These insights emphasize automating routine tasks to empower providers, enhance patient outcomes, and drive preventive care.

MediForge Vision

At Work Chop, Yinka channeled this expertise into leading the development of MediForge, a custom platform that automates clinical workflows with secure patient profiles, ICD-11-integrated diagnostics, e-prescriptions, and billing in Canadian dollars. Its offline-first design supports real-world clinic operations, enabling independent doctors and primary care practices in Canada to streamline operations, ensure compliance, and focus on patient-centered care.

Under Yinka's leadership, Work Chop delivers cutting-edge solutions that blend technical prowess with practical empathy, revolutionizing how healthcare professionals work and thrive.
