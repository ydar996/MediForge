📋 How to Assign Patients to Beds

  1. Create an Admission: First, a patient must be admitted (use the "Admit Patient" button on clinical notes, or create a new admission below).
  2. Assign Room/Bed: Once admitted, the patient appears in "Pending Admissions" section below. Click the "Assign Room/Bed" button to assign them to a specific room and bed.
  3. View Assigned: After assignment, the patient moves to the "Assigned Admissions" section with their room/bed information.
