Appointment Reminder Settings

Configure when and how appointment reminders are sent to doctors and patients.

How reminders work: Reminders are sent as in-app notifications when staff or patients log in.
  • First reminder at your chosen lead time (e.g. 14 days before)
  • Second reminder 7 days before the appointment
  • In the final 7 days: a reminder every day (if enabled)
before
First reminder will be sent this many days before each appointment.
When enabled, both doctor and patient receive a reminder every day for the week leading up to the appointment.
