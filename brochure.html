Why MediForge?

MediForge is built for Canadian physician practices and clinics—from solo providers to multi-site groups. Secure documentation, billing in Canadian dollars, and workflows that keep working when connectivity drops.

Offline-first architecture means your staff can document, prescribe, and manage patients even when the internet is down. Billing in CAD by default, with multi-currency capability when you need it. ICD-11 integration keeps you aligned with international standards. And role-based access ensures the right people see the right data—whether you run a single clinic or a multi-facility network.

Product Overview

MediForge is a full-stack Electronic Health Record (EHR) and practice management system that combines:

Clinical Documentation

SOAP notes, e-prescriptions, lab and imaging orders, specialist referrals

Laboratory & Radiology

40+ lab templates, X-ray/ultrasound/CT/MRI imaging orders, scientist workflow, external lab integration

Pharmacy Operations

Dispensing, allergy checks, invoicing

Financial Management

Invoicing, payments, cash register, and reporting in CAD—with multi-currency support available when required

Patient Engagement

Secure patient portal for appointments, medications, and results

Inpatient Care

Admissions, rounds, discharge summaries

The system runs as a Progressive Web App (PWA)—installable on any device, works offline, and syncs automatically when connectivity returns.

4. Laboratory Module

A complete laboratory workflow from order to result. The lab scientist dashboard provides a central queue of pending orders with patient search and status tracking. Doctors order tests directly from the patient record; lab scientists enter results using structured templates with field validation and normal ranges. Results flow back to the doctor's view for instant review. External lab orders support sending specimens to outside laboratories, with status tracking. Lab order billing ensures payment confirmation before result entry, integrating billing seamlessly with the lab workflow.

6. Specialist Referrals

Generate and manage referral letters with urgency levels, specialist assignment, and clinical context. Maintain a specialist directory (name, specialty, contact) for quick selection. Referrals are saved to patient records, downloadable as images, and printable for handoff. Track referral status and follow-up.

7. Pharmacy

The pharmacy module delivers a professional dispensing workflow. The pharmacy dashboard gives pharmacists a dedicated view to manage prescriptions, dispense medications, and track inventory. The accountant dashboard handles pharmacy invoicing and billing for in-house or external pharmacy operations. Before dispensing, the system performs a patient allergy check to prevent adverse events. The integrated drug database provides medication lookup with drug interaction checking, ensuring safe prescribing and dispensing.

8. Inpatient / Admissions

Full inpatient management for hospitals and facilities with beds. The admissions dashboard gives an overview of all admitted patients across wards. The admission form captures admitting diagnosis, bed assignment, and admission details. The inpatient dashboard provides bed management, rounds documentation, and vital signs tracking for inpatients. Generate discharge summaries for handoff and continuity of care. Configure inpatient facilities—define beds, wards, and rooms—and enable or disable the inpatient module per organization.

9. Appointments & Scheduling

Streamline clinic scheduling with appointment management—create, edit, and cancel appointments with patient and provider assignment. The schedule calendar offers daily, weekly, and monthly views for easy planning. Clinic schedule configuration lets you set operating hours, appointment slots, and availability. Doctor assignment manages which providers work which slots. Visit duration tracking records actual vs. scheduled appointment length for capacity planning.

11. Patient Portal

Give patients secure self-service access. The patient dashboard shows upcoming appointments, active medications, recent results, and total visits. My profile lets patients view and update demographics and contact information. Patients can view and manage appointments, see current medications, access lab & imaging results, and review their medical summary. Configure portal access and invitation workflow per organization.

12. Reports & Analytics

Data-driven insights for clinical and operational decisions. Reports provide patient and operational analytics with export. Preventive gaps summary identifies patients with gaps in preventive care (e.g., overdue screenings). Conditions breakdown and condition stats offer condition-level analytics and prevalence. Blood group summary supports inventory and transfusion planning. Vital signs analysis shows trends and patterns. CSV export for patients and reports enables external analysis and migration.

13. Security, Audit & Compliance

Enterprise-grade security and compliance. The audit log tracks user activity—logins, data changes, and access. The security dashboard provides an overview and alerts. Encryption setup & recovery offers optional field-level encryption for sensitive data with secure key recovery. Legal agreements support terms, privacy, and consent. The organization code enables secure staff registration—new users join your organization by entering the code.

14. Data Management & Backup

Protect and control your data. Download backup exports your data for backup or migration. Request backup download supports approval workflows for requested exports. Restore from backup enables disaster recovery. Import/export data supports onboarding and transitions. Storage usage monitoring tracks usage and alerts when limits approach.

15. Technical & Platform

Progressive Web App (PWA)—installable on desktop, tablet, and smartphone; no app store required. Offline capability—works without internet; data syncs automatically when connectivity returns. Cloud sync for real-time collaboration and backup. Multi-tenant—organization-level data isolation. Responsive design—optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop. ICD-11 integration for international disease classification. Integrated vaccine, drug, and allergy databases for accurate clinical documentation.
