🏥 What is MediForge?

MediForge is a secure, browser-based health record and clinic management system. Doctors, nurses, reception, billing staff — and optionally pharmacists, lab scientists, and hospital teams — work from one place. Each clinic’s data stays separate and private.

Web-based — no install required

  • Works in Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox
  • Desktop, tablet, and phone
  • Can install as an app (PWA)

Canada-first design

  • Canadian registration & postal codes
  • ICD-10-CA diagnosis codes
  • Health Canada drug search
  • Ontario lab (L) and imaging fee codes

Multi-clinic platform

  • Each organization isolated
  • Staff join with org code
  • Role-based dashboards
  • Platform owner oversight
