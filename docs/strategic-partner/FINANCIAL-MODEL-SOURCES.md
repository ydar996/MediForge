# Financial Model: Sourced Figures and TBD Items

**Purpose:** Diligence models must not show dollar amounts without a source. This document lists what is **sourced**, what is **term-sheet contractual**, what uses **industry benchmark estimates** (clearly labeled), and what stays **TBD until vendor quotes** arrive.

## Partner expectations: commitment vs deploy

**Maximum commitment** is the term-sheet ceiling (**$300k–$600k**). **Expected deploy** is what we plan to use in a given scenario. They are not the same number.

| Concept | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **Max commitment** | Upper limit the Strategic Partner agrees to fund over ~18 months (milestone tranches) |
| **Expected deploy** | Realistic planning estimate for Lean / Base / Stress (includes dev fee + cert + GTM buffer) |
| **Sourced line items** | OntarioMD fees, term-sheet dev fee, legal cap: shown with dollar amounts |
| **TBD line items** | Audit, consulting, pilots: category named, amount blank until quotes exist |
| **Remaining tranche balance (awaiting quotes)** | Headroom left in a tranche after sourced items; **not** a planned invoice |

**Tranches 3 and 4** release only when Phase B/C milestones are met **and** the partner approves an updated use-of-proceeds budget (vendor quotes where applicable).

### Lean / Base / Stress scenarios

| Scenario | Max commitment | New cert + pilot spend | Expected total deploy* | Notes |
|----------|----------------|------------------------|-------------------------|-------|
| **Lean** | **$300k** | **$60k–$90k** | **$250k–$300k** | Tight control; founder-heavy; minimal paid consulting |
| **Base (default planning)** | **$400k** | **$90k–$150k** | **$300k–$400k** | Default model midpoint; benchmark cert + dev fee + modest GTM |
| **Stress (ceiling)** | **$600k** | **$150k–$200k+** | **$450k–$600k** | High audit/remediation; ceiling for modeling, not a spending target |

*Expected deploy includes founder development fee and commercialization buffer. Cert spend excludes sunk Phases 0–8 build. OntarioMD **$27,500 + HST** is sourced in all scenarios.

### What we tell the partner

Work Chop Inc. is seeking a seed commitment of **up to CAD $400,000**.

Capital deploys in four milestone-linked tranches over ~18 months (25% / 25% / 30% / 20%).

The discussion-term sheet range is **CAD $300,000 – $600,000**.

Under our **Base planning scenario**, we expect to deploy approximately **CAD $300,000 – $400,000** in total.

That includes the founder development fee, certification, and a modest commercialization buffer.

It does not fund greenfield EMR build. Phases 0–8 software is already delivered.

**Sourced:** OntarioMD validation fees **CAD $27,500 + HST** (~**$31,075** per published schedule).

**Benchmark (pending quotes):** new cert-related spend **$90,000 – $150,000** (audit, legal, consulting, reference site, pilots).

Unquoted categories stay **TBD** in tranche tables. Remaining balance is headroom, not a committed line item.

**Tranches 3 and 4** require Phase B/C milestones **and** partner approval of an updated use-of-proceeds schedule.

Deployment above Base, or toward the **$600,000** Stress ceiling, requires mutual agreement.

The Stress figure is a diligence ceiling, not our spending target.

## Industry benchmark estimates (June 2026 research)

**Disclaimer:** Planning ranges only. Obtain formal quotes before assigning tranche line items.

| Category | Benchmark (CAD) | Basis |
|----------|-----------------|-------|
| PIA / third-party security audit / PHIPA-aligned pen test | **$10k–$40k** | Industry compliance benchmarks |
| Legal / PHIPA pack finalization | **$5k–$15k** | Beyond term-sheet company legal cap where applicable |
| Consulting / application and evidence support | **$5k–$25k+** | OntarioMD path navigation |
| Reference / pilot clinic deployment (Stage 5 + Phase C) | **$5k–$30k+** | Reference site and first pilot support |
| Dev remediation / sandbox / minor alignment | **Variable** (potentially low) | Depends on Stage 5 validation findings |
| Provincial onboarding | **Mostly time + support contracts** | Low direct enrollment fees |
| **Rough total additional to cert + initial live readiness** | **~$60k–$200k+** (Base mid **~$90k–$150k**) | See Lean / Base / Stress table above |

Interactive tranche tables show **TBD** for unquoted categories. **Remaining tranche balance (awaiting quotes)** is the mathematical remainder per tranche after sourced items.
