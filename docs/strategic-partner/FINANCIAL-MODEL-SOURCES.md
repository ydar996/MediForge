## Industry benchmark estimates (June 2026 research)

**Disclaimer:** These are **planning ranges**, not vendor quotes. Obtain formal quotes before budgeting tranche line items. Validation findings may shift remediation spend.

| Category | Benchmark (CAD) | Basis |
|----------|-----------------|-------|
| PIA / third-party security audit / PHIPA-aligned pen test | **$10k–$40k** | Industry compliance benchmarks; focused scope given existing PHIPA pack and audit trails |
| Legal / PHIPA pack finalization | **$5k–$15k** | Beyond term-sheet company legal cap where applicable |
| Consulting / application and evidence support | **$5k–$25k+** | OntarioMD path navigation (Mindbowser, n.d.; OntarioMD, n.d.-b) |
| Reference / pilot clinic deployment (Stage 5 + Phase C) | **$5k–$30k+** | Reference site and first pilot support |
| Dev remediation / sandbox / minor alignment | **Variable** (potentially low) | Depends on Stage 5 validation findings |
| Provincial onboarding (MCEDT, OLIS, PrescribeIT, DIR, HRM, DHDR) | **Mostly time + support contracts** | Low direct enrollment fees; process-driven |
| **Rough total additional from current state to cert + initial live readiness** | **~$60k–$200k+** (mid **~$80k–$150k**) | Excludes sunk Phases 0–8 build; aligns with remaining seed tranche capacity |

Interactive tranche tables on `/financial-model` still show **TBD** for unquoted categories until owner enters vendor quotes. Benchmarks appear on `/ontario-readiness#certification-path` and `/project-plan` for diligence planning.

## Scenario assumptions (owner-entered, not market quotes)

These fields on `/financial-model` are **editable scenario inputs** for revenue projection only. They are **not** presented as industry benchmarks until validated:

- Monthly subscription per provider
- Setup fee per new clinic
- Onboarding cost per clinic
- Year-by-year OpEx and clinic count

---

## TBD pending vendor / owner quotes

Do **not** show fixed single dollar amounts in tranche outflows for these until quotes or invoices exist:

- Provincial onboarding enrollment and conformance testing (beyond OntarioMD published fees)
- Third-party privacy impact assessment and security audit (use benchmark range for planning only)
- Reference clinic and Stage 5 validation support
- Pilot clinic support, sales, and commercial launch spend
- Travel and integration engineering beyond internal capacity

Tranche tables show **deployment amount** (from seed % × commitment) minus **sourced line items** = **unallocated pending quotes**.
