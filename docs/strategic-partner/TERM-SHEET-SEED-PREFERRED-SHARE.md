## 1. Company Overview and Valuation

The Company has developed a proprietary Electronic Medical Records (EMR) software platform for physicians, clinics, and hospitals. The Product is **deployed and in use** as a multi-clinic platform with **Phases 0–8 Ontario EMR readiness software complete** where possible (documented readiness **72–82%**). Live provincial pipes and OntarioMD Stage 5 remain credential-gated.

**Pre-money valuation:** CAD **$1,500,000 – $2,200,000** (target ~$1,800,000; negotiable).

*Justification: Grounded in replacement cost of platform + market comps for early healthtech SaaS. Pre-seed norms $500k–$5M; healthtech premium for compliance. Defensible via cost approach ($80k–$350k+ build cost) + scorecard/Berkus method for working product + market opportunity.*

## 4. Upfront Development Fee

Upon Closing, the Company shall pay the Founder a one-time development fee of **CAD $80,000 – $120,000**. This recognizes the Founder's development of the deployed platform (Phases 0–8 software) and supports the equity allocation. Payment structure per [Valuation and Equity Structure](VALUATION-AND-EQUITY-STRUCTURE.md) (minimum 40% at closing; balance per negotiated note, conversion, or hybrid).

---

## 5. Intellectual Property and Contributions

The Founder assigns all right, title, and interest in the Product, code, designs, and IP to the Company. The Founder represents sole development (or proper assignments) with no encumbrances.

---

## 6. Governance and Control

- **Board:** Initially 3 members: Founder (CEO/Chair), 1 mutually agreed independent/advisor, and up to 1 Strategic Partner seat or observer.
- **Control:** Founder retains majority voting control via Common Shares and operational authority as CEO.
- **Protective provisions:** Strategic Partner receives standard veto on major corporate actions. Day-to-day and product decisions remain with Founder/Board majority.
- **Information rights:** Standard terms apply.
