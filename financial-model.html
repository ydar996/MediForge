Default assumptions

What does the $300k–$600k seed outlay comprise?

The Strategic Partner maximum commitment is $300k–$600k over ~18 months (milestone tranches). Our default planning scenario is Base (~$400k expected deploy), not the $600k ceiling. Phases 0–8 platform software is already delivered; seed funds certification, live pipes, pilots, and go-to-market.

The founder development fee ($80k–$120k) is a separate obligation recognizing work already delivered; it is not part of the cert-only budget.

Evidence policy: Tranche outflows show dollar amounts only when sourced (term sheet or published fee schedule). Other categories show TBD until quotes. The remaining tranche balance (awaiting quotes) row is headroom in each tranche, not a planned expense.
Revenue inputs below are editable scenario assumptions (pricing, OpEx, clinic growth), not industry benchmarks.
