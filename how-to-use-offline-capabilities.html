How to Use Offline Capabilities

MediForge can keep working when your internet connection is unreliable or temporarily unavailable. This guide explains how to use the offline features so you can continue documenting, prescribing, and managing patients even when the connection drops.

← Back to Brochure

📋 Before You Go Offline

To use MediForge offline, you need to prepare once while you have a good connection:

1. Log in while online

  • Open MediForge in your browser and log in as usual.
  • Your session must be established while you are online.

2. Visit the pages you use most

  • Open the main pages you work with: Dashboard, Patients, Patient Details, Clinical Note, Appointments, Billing, etc.
  • Each page you open is stored for offline use.
  • The more pages you open while online, the more you can do later when offline.

3. Use a supported browser

  • Use a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox) on your computer, tablet, or phone.
  • Keep the MediForge tab or window open (or return to it) when you expect to work offline.

📴 When You Are Offline

Once you have logged in and loaded the app while online, you can continue working when the connection is lost:

What you can do offline

View patients Open your patient list and patient records.
Add or edit patients New or updated information is saved and will sync when you are back online.
Document clinical notes Write and save notes; they will sync when the connection returns.
Prescribe medications Create prescriptions; they will sync when you are back online.
Manage appointments Add or edit appointments; changes will sync later.
Record payments Payment data is saved locally and syncs when you reconnect.
Enter lab results Lab data is stored locally and syncs when online.

What you will see

  • A status message may appear indicating that you are offline or that data will sync when the connection is restored.
  • Any changes you make are saved locally and will be sent to the server automatically once you are back online.
  • You do not need to do anything special; syncing happens in the background.

🔄 When You Are Back Online

  • Reconnect to the internet as usual.
  • Open or refresh MediForge.
  • Your recent changes will sync automatically.
  • You can continue working as normal once sync completes.

💡 Tips for Reliable Offline Use

  1. Prepare before field visits – Log in and open the main pages you need before leaving a location with good internet.
  2. Use the same device – Offline data is stored on the device you use. Use the same computer, tablet, or phone for consistency.
  3. Check the status indicator – If the app shows an offline or “will sync when online” message, your changes are being saved and will sync later.
  4. Avoid logging out offline – If possible, stay logged in until you are back online so your session remains valid.

⚠️ Limitations

  • First-time setup – You must log in at least once while online.
  • New devices – On a new device or browser, you need to log in and load the app while online before offline use will work.
  • Real-time updates – While offline, you will not see changes made by other users until you are back online and sync completes.
  • Some features – A few features that depend on a live connection may not be available until you are back online.
