The short version

MediForge is a working multi-clinic EMR, not a prototype. We completed Phase 0 (internal evidence) and Phase 1 (core standards hardening) in June 2026. We are not OntarioMD-certified yet: that gate requires the provincial vendor process and live partner credentials every certified EMR must obtain.

Phase 0 highlights

  • OntarioMD gap report and implementation plan
  • PHIPA compliance documentation pack
  • FHIR R4 patient chart export and CPP-aligned summary
  • Structured consent capture and i4C indicator mapping
  • OHIP claim file draft export and investor readiness page

Phase 1 highlights

  • HL7 v2 and FHIR R4 libraries tested; gateway exposes Bundle export
  • DICOMweb client and imaging FHIR builders
  • Append-only audit for chart access and integration messages
  • Organization-wide consent management module
  • Clinical image/PDF viewer for chart attachments

Why this matters

Daily clinic workflows are largely built. HL7, FHIR, and DICOMweb are implemented modules with tests, not roadmap slides. Diligence materials are live at the links below.

What comes next

OntarioMD vendor path, MCEDT claims, OLIS labs, PrescribeIT, and provincial imaging integration: in that priority order, as partner credentials allow.
