Enable end-to-end encryption for your organization's patient data

Why Enable Encryption?

  • HIPAA/GDPR Compliance: Required for handling protected health information
  • Data Security: Patient data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Privacy Protection: Only authorized users with the master password can decrypt data
  • Backward Compatible: Existing data will be encrypted gradually as it's accessed
