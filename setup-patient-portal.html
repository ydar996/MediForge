Setup Patient Portal Access

Purpose: Create patient portal login credentials for your patients.

What this does:
  • Searches for patients by name or patient ID
  • Shows which patients already have portal access
  • Creates portal login credentials (username & temporary password)
  • Generates credentials that you can send to patients via email or SMS
  • Patients will be required to change password on first login
Duplicate records: If the same person appears in multiple patient records, we show one consolidated card. We prefer the record that already has portal access, or the lowest patient ID. Use View in Patient Details to verify you have the correct record before creating portal access.
