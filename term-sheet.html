1. Company Overview and Valuation

Work Chop Inc. has developed MediForge, a proprietary EMR platform for physicians, clinics, and hospitals. The product is deployed and in active use as a multi-clinic platform (not a prototype). Phases 0–8 Ontario EMR readiness software are complete where possible (documented readiness 72–82%). Live provincial pipes and OntarioMD Stage 5 validation remain partner- and credential-gated.

Pre-money valuation: CAD $1,500,000 – $2,200,000 (target ~$1,800,000; negotiable). See Valuation & Equity Structure for full justification.

4. Upfront Development Fee

Upon Closing, the Company shall pay the Founder a one-time development fee of CAD $80,000–$120,000. Minimum 40% at Closing = $32,000–$48,000 cash; deferred balance $48,000–$72,000 per options below (8–12% note interest; 15–25% convertible discount).

5. Intellectual Property

The Founder assigns all right, title, and interest in the Product, code, designs, and IP to the Company, with no encumbrances.

6. Governance and Control

  • Board: Initially 3 members: Founder (CEO/Chair), 1 mutually agreed independent/advisor, and up to 1 Strategic Partner seat or observer.
  • Control: Founder retains majority voting control via Common Shares and operational authority as CEO.
  • Protective provisions: Strategic Partner receives standard veto on major corporate actions (e.g., sale of the Company or significant new debt). Day-to-day and product decisions remain with Founder/Board majority.
  • Information rights: Standard terms apply.
